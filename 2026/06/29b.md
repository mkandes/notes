### 3. Ticket Data Discussion
  - **[Subject](https://developer.zendesk.com/api-reference/ticketing/tickets/tickets/#subject)**: Subject field is required and can be up to 255 characters. It's typically included in the support request submitted by the requester. For example, when someone submits a support request through email, the subject line of the email is used as the ticket's subject.
  - **[Description](https://developer.zendesk.com/api-reference/ticketing/tickets/tickets/#description-and-first-comment)**: Description field is required. The description is the first comment in the ticket, whether it's public or internal. When a user submits an email support request, the body of the email request is used as the description.
  - **[Comments](https://developer.zendesk.com/api-reference/ticketing/tickets/ticket_comments/)**: Ticket comments represent the conversation between requesters, collaborators, and agents. Comments can be public or private.
