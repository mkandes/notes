### 2. Ticket Data Discussion
- Show-and-tell [Zendesk ticket](https://developer.zendesk.com/api-reference/ticketing/tickets/tickets/) data exploration notebook.
- Explain the 3 datasets
  - **Subject** (500): Subject field is required and can be up to 255 characters. It's typically included in the support request submitted by the requester. For example, when someone submits a support request through email, the subject line of the email is used as the ticket's subject.
  - **Description** (500): Description field is required. The description is the first comment in the ticket, whether it's public or internal. When a user submits an email support request, the body of the email request is used as the description.
  - **[Comments](https://developer.zendesk.com/api-reference/ticketing/tickets/ticket_comments/)** (500): Ticket comments represent the conversation between requesters, collaborators, and agents. Comments can be public or private.
