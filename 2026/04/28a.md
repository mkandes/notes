- Step 1: Launch `AraRAN-OAI-BaseStation-000.yaml` on `CurtissFarm-AraRAN-OAI-BaseStation-000`
- Step 2: Launch `AraRAN-OAI-UserEquipment-XXX.yaml` on `CurtissFarm-AraRAN-OAI-UserEquipment-001`
