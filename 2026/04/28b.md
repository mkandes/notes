- Step 2: Launch `AraRAN-OAI-BaseStation-000.yaml` on `CurtissFarm-AraRAN-OAI-BaseStation-000`
  - Note: Must change 10.0.4.4 to 10.0.4.3
- Step 3: Launch `AraRAN-OAI-UserEquipment-XXX.yaml` on `CurtissFarm-AraRAN-OAI-UserEquipment-001`
