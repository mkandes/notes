# Outdoor Over-The-Air (OTA) OpenAirInterface 5G SA Experiment (using a Self-Deployed Core Network at 10.0.4.3 on Ara_Shared_Net)

- Step 0: Launch `AraRAN-OAI-CoreNetwork-00X.yaml` on `DataCenter-AraRAN-OAI-CoreNetwork-001`
- Step 1: Launch `AraRAN-OAI-BaseStation-000.yaml` on `CurtissFarm-AraRAN-OAI-BaseStation-000`; note: Must change 10.0.4.4 to 10.0.4.3
- Step 2: Launch `AraRAN-OAI-UserEquipment-XXX.yaml` on `CurtissFarm-AraRAN-OAI-UserEquipment-001`
- Step 3: Test the reachability of the 5G Core from the gNB container, run a ping in the gNB container toward the AMF of the core network.
